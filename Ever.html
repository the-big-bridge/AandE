If I Ever Write About Philosophy

2022'0337

If I ever write about philosophy, I will make it enjoyable.

My words will either be moving and sublime, feels like California sunshine;

or it will be deep and savoring, tastes like structured fine wine.

Whenever I can express myself in one hundred words, I will not torture anyone with one hundred and one;

Because I remember how I read Hegel and Kant, it could have been a lot more fun.

I live in the heyday of modernity, an explosion of diversity, those and some more details I will put in the preface;

Saving the scholars some effort of guessing what influence I am under, and myself some reface.
