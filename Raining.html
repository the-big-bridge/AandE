Raining

2022'1001

The rain is not a rain, it is a conference.

The sound it makes is water’s language.

They have traveled from north to south,

And now they are sharing their stories.


This one has been in champaign glasses,

That one has been in New York’s sewer,

This one has been the saliva shared by lovers,

That one has been the tear of a mother.


Every one of them is passionate,

They cannot wait to share their story,

They all speak together,

Then it became lyrics.


People stand under the roof,

Annoyed and complaining,

They heard the entire rain,

But understood nothing.
